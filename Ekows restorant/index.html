About Us

Brief history of the restaurant, including its founding and notable achievements.

  • Chef 1

    Chef Ekow Coleman is a renowned Ghanaian chef based in Sunyani, known for his innovative and traditional culinary creations. With a passion for showcasing the rich flavors and ingredients of Ghanaian cuisine, Chef Ekow has built a reputation for delivering exceptional dining experiences. His dishes often blend traditional Ghanaian flavors with modern twists, making him a favorite among locals and visitors alike. As a proud ambassador of Ghanaian cuisine, Chef Ekow continues to inspire and delight food enthusiasts in Sunyani and beyond.
